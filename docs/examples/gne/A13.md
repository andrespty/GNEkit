# Problem A13

The **river basin pollution game** taken from Krawczyk and Uryasev (2000). The Jaco-Hessian is constant and symmetric positive definite.

**Players:** $N = 3$, each controlling a single variable $x^\nu \in \mathbb{R}$.

## Objective Functions

$$\theta_\nu(\mathbf{x}) := x^\nu\bigl(c_{1\nu} + c_{2\nu}\,x^\nu - d_1 + d_2(x^1 + x^2 + x^3)\bigr), \quad \nu = 1,2,3$$

with $d_1 = 3$, $d_2 = 0.01$.

## Joint Constraints

$$u_{11}e_1 x^1 + u_{21}e_2 x^2 + u_{31}e_3 x^3 \leq K_1$$

$$u_{12}e_1 x^1 + u_{22}e_2 x^2 + u_{32}e_3 x^3 \leq K_2$$

with $K_1 = K_2 = 100$.

| Player $\nu$ | $c_{1\nu}$ | $c_{2\nu}$ | $e_\nu$ | $u_{\nu 1}$ | $u_{\nu 2}$ |
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
| 1 | 0.10 | 0.01 | 0.50 | 6.5 | 4.583 |
| 2 | 0.12 | 0.05 | 0.25 | 5.0 | 6.250 |
| 3 | 0.15 | 0.01 | 0.75 | 5.5 | 3.750 |

## Known Solution

$$\bar{x} \approx (21.147,\; 16.028,\; 2.724)^T$$
