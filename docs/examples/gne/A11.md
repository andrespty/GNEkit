# Problem A11

A simple jointly convex GNEP taken from Facchinei, Fischer, and Piccialli (2009) with **infinitely many solutions** but a unique normalized equilibrium. The Jaco-Hessian is symmetric positive definite everywhere.

**Players:** $N = 2$, each controlling a single variable $x^\nu \in \mathbb{R}$.

## Optimization Problems

$$\min_{x^1}\; (x^1 - 1)^2 \qquad \text{s.t.} \quad x^1 + x^2 \leq 1$$

$$\min_{x^2}\; \left(x^2 - \tfrac{1}{2}\right)^2 \qquad \text{s.t.} \quad x^1 + x^2 \leq 1$$

## Known Solutions

The GNEP has infinitely many solutions:

$$\{\,(\alpha,\; 1-\alpha)^T \mid \alpha \in [1/2,\, 1]\,\}$$

The unique **normalized equilibrium** is $\bar{x} = (3/4,\; 1/4)^T$.
