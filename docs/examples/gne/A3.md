# Problem A3

A three-player quadratic GNEP with linear coupling constraints. The Jaco-Hessian is constant but indefinite.

**Players:** $N = 3$ with $n_1 = 3$, $n_2 = 2$, $n_3 = 2$ variables respectively.

## Objective Functions

Each player has a strongly convex quadratic objective:

$$\theta_\nu(\mathbf{x}) = \tfrac{1}{2}(x^\nu)^T A^\nu x^\nu + (x^\nu)^T\\!(B^\nu x^{-\nu} + b^\nu)$$

with matrices:

$$A^1 = \begin{pmatrix} 20 & 5 & 3 \\ 5 & 5 & -5 \\ 3 & -5 & 15 \end{pmatrix}, \quad A^2 = \begin{pmatrix} 11 & -1 \\ -1 & 9 \end{pmatrix}, \quad A^3 = \begin{pmatrix} 48 & 39 \\ 39 & 53 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$B^1 = \begin{pmatrix} -6 & 10 & 11 & 20 \\ 10 & -4 & -17 & 9 \\ 15 & 8 & -22 & 21 \end{pmatrix}, \quad b^1 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$B^2 = \begin{pmatrix} 20 & 1 & -3 & 12 & 1 \\ 10 & -4 & 8 & 16 & 21 \end{pmatrix}, \quad b^2 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$B^3 = \begin{pmatrix} 10 & -2 & 22 & 12 & 16 \\ 9 & 19 & 21 & -4 & 20 \end{pmatrix}, \quad b^3 = \begin{pmatrix} -1 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}$$

## Constraints

**Bound constraints (all players):** $-10 \leq x \leq 10$

**Player 1 (linear coupling):**

$$x^1_1 + x^1_2 + x^1_3 \leq 20, \quad x^1_1 + x^1_2 - x^1_3 \leq x^2_1 - x^3_2 + 5$$

**Player 2:**

$$x^2_1 - x^2_2 \leq x^1_2 + x^1_3 - x^3_1 + 7$$

**Player 3:**

$$x^3_2 \leq x^1_1 + x^1_3 - x^2_1 + 4$$

## Known Solution

$$\bar{x} \approx (-0.3805,\; -0.1227,\; -0.9932,\; 0.3903,\; 1.1639,\; 0.0504,\; 0.0176)^T$$
