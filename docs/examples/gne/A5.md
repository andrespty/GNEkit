# Problem A5

Same structure as Problem A3 but with different data matrices that yield a **constant and positive definite** Jaco-Hessian.

**Players:** $N = 3$ with $n_1 = 3$, $n_2 = 2$, $n_3 = 2$ variables respectively.

## Objective Functions

$$\theta_\nu(\mathbf{x}) = \tfrac{1}{2}(x^\nu)^T A^\nu x^\nu + (x^\nu)^T\\!(B^\nu x^{-\nu} + b^\nu)$$

$$A^1 = \begin{pmatrix} 20 & 6 & 0 \\ 6 & 6 & -1 \\ 0 & -1 & 8 \end{pmatrix}, \quad A^2 = \begin{pmatrix} 11 & 1 \\ 1 & 7 \end{pmatrix}, \quad A^3 = \begin{pmatrix} 28 & 14 \\ 14 & 29 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$B^1 = \begin{pmatrix} -1 & -2 & -4 & -3 \\ 0 & -3 & 0 & -4 \\ 0 & 1 & 9 & 6 \end{pmatrix}, \quad b^1 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$B^2 = \begin{pmatrix} -1 & 0 & 0 & -7 & 4 \\ -2 & -3 & 1 & 4 & 11 \end{pmatrix}, \quad b^2 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$B^3 = \begin{pmatrix} -4 & 0 & 9 & -7 & 4 \\ -3 & -4 & 6 & 4 & 11 \end{pmatrix}, \quad b^3 = \begin{pmatrix} -1 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}$$

## Constraints

**Bound constraints:** lower bounds all $0$, upper bounds same as Problem A3.

**Coupling constraints:** same as Problem A3.
