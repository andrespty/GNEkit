# Problem A16

An **oligopoly model** from Outrata, Kocvara, and Zowe (1998), based on a standard NEP by Murphy, Sherali, and Soyster (1982). The Jaco-Hessian is non-constant but positive definite at the solutions.

**Players:** $N = 5$, each controlling a single variable $x^\nu \in \mathbb{R}$.

## Objective Functions

$$\theta_\nu(x) := f_\nu(x^\nu) - 5000^{1/\gamma}\,x^\nu(x^1 + \cdots + x^N)^{-1/\gamma}, \quad \nu = 1, \ldots, N$$

with $\gamma = 1.1$ and:

$$f_\nu(x^\nu) := c_\nu x^\nu + \frac{\delta_\nu}{1+\delta_\nu}K_\nu^{-1/\delta_\nu}(x^\nu)^{(1+\delta_\nu)/\delta_\nu}$$

## Constraints

$$x^1 + \cdots + x^N \leq P, \quad x^\nu \geq 0 \quad \forall\, \nu$$

## Parameters

| $\nu$ | $c_\nu$ | $K_\nu$ | $\delta_\nu$ |
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
| 1 | 10 | 5 | 1.2 |
| 2 | 8 | 5 | 1.1 |
| 3 | 6 | 5 | 1.0 |
| 4 | 4 | 5 | 0.9 |
| 5 | 2 | 5 | 0.8 |
