# Problem A6

A variant of Problem A3 featuring **nonlinear coupling constraints** and $x$-dependent matrices. The Jaco-Hessian is neither constant nor positive (semi-)definite in general.

**Players:** $N = 3$ with $n_1 = 3$, $n_2 = 2$, $n_3 = 2$ variables. All variables bounded in $[1, 10]$.

## Objective Functions

$$\theta_\nu(\mathbf{x}) = \tfrac{1}{2}(x^\nu)^T A^\nu x^\nu + (x^\nu)^T\\!(B^\nu x^{-\nu} + b^\nu)$$

with $x$-dependent matrices:

$$A^1 = \begin{pmatrix} 20+(x^2_1)^2 & 5 & 3 \\ 5 & 5+(x^2_2)^2 & -5 \\ 3 & -5 & 15 \end{pmatrix}, \quad A^2 = \begin{pmatrix} 11+(x^3_1)^2 & -1 \\ -1 & 9 \end{pmatrix}, \quad A^3 = \begin{pmatrix} 48 & 39 \\ 39 & 53+(x^1_1)^2 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$b^1 = (1,\,-2,\,-3)^T, \quad b^2 = (1,\,2)^T, \quad b^3 = (1,\,-2)^T$$

## Constraints

**Bound constraints (all players):** $1 \leq x \leq 10$

**Player 1 (nonlinear coupling):**

$$x^1_1 + x^1_2 + x^1_3 \leq 20, \quad x^1_1 + x^1_2 - x^1_3 \leq x^2_1 - x^3_2 + 3.7, \quad (x^1_1)^4 + x^3_1 x^1_2 \leq x^2_1 + 2$$

**Player 2:**

$$x^2_1 - x^2_2 \leq x^1_2 + x^1_3 - x^3_1 + 7, \quad (x^2_1 - 2)^2 + (x^2_2)^2 \leq 0.75 + (x^1_1)^2$$

**Player 3:**

$$x^3_2 \leq x^1_1 + x^1_3 - x^2_1 + 4, \quad 2(x^3_1)^2 - (x^3_2 - 2)^2 \leq x^2_1 x^3_1 + 1.5$$

## Known Solution

$$\bar{x} \approx (0.9999,\; 2.3157,\; 0.9999,\; 1.3150,\; 0.9999,\; 0.9999,\; 1.0971)^T$$
