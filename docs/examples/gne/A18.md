# Problem A18

An **electricity market model** originally proposed by Pang and Fukushima (2005) and further studied by Nabetani, Tseng, and Fukushima (2008). Two electricity companies compete across three regional nodes.

**Players:** $N = 2$. Player 1 controls $(x_1, \ldots, x_6)$; player 2 controls $(x_7, \ldots, x_{12})$.

## Price Functions

$$S_1 := 40 - \tfrac{40}{500}(x_1+x_4+x_7+x_{10}), \quad S_2 := 35 - \tfrac{35}{400}(x_2+x_5+x_8+x_{11})$$

$$S_3 := 32 - \tfrac{32}{600}(x_3+x_6+x_9+x_{12})$$

## Objective Functions

$$\theta_1(x) := (15-S_1)(x_1+x_4) + (15-S_2)(x_2+x_5) + (15-S_3)(x_3+x_6)$$

$$\theta_2(x) := (15-S_1)(x_7+x_{10}) + (15-S_2)(x_8+x_{11}) + (15-S_3)(x_9+x_{12})$$

## Constraints

**Both players share the joint constraints:**

$$S_j - S_i \leq 1 \quad \forall\, i,j \in \{1,2,3\},\; i \neq j$$

**Player 1** additionally:

$$x_1, \ldots, x_6 \geq 0, \quad x_1+x_2+x_3 \leq 100, \quad x_4+x_5+x_6 \leq 50$$

**Player 2** additionally:

$$x_7, \ldots, x_{12} \geq 0, \quad x_7+x_8+x_9 \leq 100, \quad x_{10}+x_{11}+x_{12} \leq 50$$
